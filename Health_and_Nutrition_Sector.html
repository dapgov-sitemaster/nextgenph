ATAY: AI Multi-Modal Tool Assessing Young Adult’s Biomarker And Factors For Metabolic Dysfunction-Associated Fatty Liver Disease (MAFLD) Risk Detection
Quezon City Science High School

“ATAY” model, designed to automatically assess liver health and MAFLD risk in young adults by analyzing liver health datasets related to NAFLD such as biomarkers, demographics, lifestyle habits, and medical history.

TRAC: Treatment Recording and Admission Care System for Hospitalized Patients
Philippine Science High School - Central Visayas Campus

TRAC is a digital prescription and medication tracking system designed strictly for admitted patients. Each patient will be provided with an ID band containing both a barcode and a QR code. The barcode will be used by healthcare workers, who can scan it with a hospital device or mobile phone to access the patient’s complete medication data, allergies, and restrictions. The QR code will be intended for patients, allowing them to quickly view their prescriptions, schedules, and dietary notes through the hospital’s website or portal.

Nutrindahan: Empowering Farmers, Nourishing Families, and Creating Smarter Access to Nutrition
Carlos Hilado Memorial State University

Nutrindahan is a digital platform combining Artificial Intelligence-powered nutrition guidance, community food mapping, and virtual coaching. To ensure seamless integration and effective implementation, the Local Nutrition Office will handle the backend data management and AI model refinement, while the Local Agriculture Office, in coordination with community health workers, will manage front-line services, ensuring direct engagement with users. This collaborative approach will harmonize with existing local and national programs, such as the Philippine Plan of Action for Nutrition and the National Feeding Program.

Konsulta Kiosk: Improving Healthcare Access for Patients and Providers through Telehealth and Digital Health Record Integration In Urban Communities
Lyceum of the Philippines University - Cavite

The Konsulta Kiosk is a solar-powered telehealth booth integrated with a Digital Health Record (DHR) system, designed to act as a bridge between barangay health centers and hospitals. It decentralizes primary consultations, digitizes patient records, and links them seamlessly to higher-level care.

Anthromax
Cagayan State University - Andrews Campus

Anthromax is an all-in-one, portable briefcase that integrates essential anthropometric tools into a single, cohesive unit. This durable, hardshell case contains a fixed digital weighing scale and a collapsible height rod. The tools automatically transmit data wirelessly to a detachable, tablet-like screen, eliminating manual data entry and reducing human error.
