NextGenPH is an open and participatory innovation contest that invites youth to design and pitch practical solutions to improve public services. The initiative aims to foster faster, more platform that values creativity, collaboration, and public impact. Innovate Ideas submitted should be grounded in personal experience or informed by the experiences of others in availing public services.
