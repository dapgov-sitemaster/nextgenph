KITA: A low-connectivity friendly AI tool that turns MSME paper logs into smart decisions to boost rural productivity and competitiveness
Ateneo de Manila University

Backed by Ateneo’s Technology Business Incubator (BUILD), KITA is the “missing-middle” layer between pen-and-paper and full ERP/CRM. It is modular, AI-assisted, and low-connectivity-tolerant, delivering the 20% of capability that moves 80% of MSME work. KITA turns paper into decisions through our framework (Capture → Compute → Decide → Connect), so micro and small operators in both the metro and rural provinces lift productivity and competitiveness without a costly system overhaul.
